Let ω(n) denote the number of distinct prime divisors of a positive
integer n.  
So ω(1) = 0 and ω(360) = ω(2^3× 3^2× 5) =
3.

Let S(n) be ∑_d | n 2^ω(d).  
E.g. S(6) = 2^ω(1)+2^ω(2)+2^ω(3)+2^ω(6) =
2^0+2^1+2^1+2^2 = 9.

Let F(n)=∑_i=2^n S(i!). F(10)=4821.

Find F(10 000 000). Give your answer modulo 1 000 000 087.
