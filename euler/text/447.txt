For every integer n>1, the family of functions f_n,a,b is defined by  
f_n,a,b(x)≡ a x + b  n for a,b,x integer and 0< a <n, 0
≤ b < n,0 ≤ x < n.

We will call f_n,a,b a _retraction_ if f_n,a,b(f_n,a,b(x))
≡ f_n,a,b(x)  n for every 0 ≤ x < n.  
Let R(n) be the number of retractions for n.

F(N)=∑_n=2^N R(n).  
F(10^7)≡ 638042271  1 000 000 007.  

Find F(10^14).  
Give your answer modulo 1 000 000 007.
