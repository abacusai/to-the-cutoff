An integer is called **B -smooth** if none of its prime factors is greater
than B.

Let S_B(n) be the largest B-smooth divisor of n.  
Examples:  
S_1(10)=1  
S_4(2100) = 12  
S_17(2496144) = 5712

Define F(n)=∑_B=1^n ∑_r=0^n S_B( n r). Here,
n r denotes the binomial coefficient.  
Examples:  
F(11) = 3132  
F(1111)  1 000 000 993 = 706036312  
F(111 111)  1 000 000 993 = 22156169

Find F(11 111 111)  1 000 000 993.
